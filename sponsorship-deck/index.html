New — Monthly

Gen AI Film Jam

Monthly · Teams compete to make short films with AI tools

Teams of creatives and technologists use ZeroSpace's LED volume, studio sets, and AI tools to produce short films in a single day. Every film produced is a real-world case study for the tools used to make it.

The pilot event (August 2025) drew 150 registrations with attendees from Netflix, ByteDance, Nasdaq, Capital One, and Harvard Business School. 42 on-site participants produced work in a single evening.

  • Credits sponsor — provide API credits (low cost, high value, real usage data)
  • Tool partner — your product featured as an official jam tool
  • Prize sponsor — fund a category award
  • Production partner — supply equipment for branding + footage rights
New — Monthly

Vibe Coding
Competition

Monthly · Live head-to-head coding battles on stage

Two builders go head-to-head, using AI coding tools to build something from scratch in 8 minutes, then present to the audience. For the first time, coding is fast enough to hold a live audience's attention.

Piloted at the Anthropic-sponsored Vibe Shift event (Oct 2025, co-produced with Rhizome and New Museum's New Inc). Think World Excel Championships meets esports.

  • Title sponsor — "The [Company] Vibe Coding Championship" (exclusive)
  • Category sponsor — own a competition track
  • Infrastructure sponsor — provide compute for competitors
  • Livestream sponsor — branded overlays and ad reads
