Generative Production Challenge

A one-day live-action and AI post-production sprint designed for the next generation of filmmakers.

Brooklyn, NY Live shoot + AI post + premiere
The future of production will be tested under real creative pressure.

Teams shoot original live-action material, move directly into AI-assisted post, and premiere finished micro-shorts the same night. Sponsors' platforms and tools become part of filmmaking process rather than just logos on a poster.

Why Now

AI tools need proof inside real production.

The market is crowded with demos. The Challenge creates live, observable adoption: filmmakers using emerging tools while solving the same constraints they face on real sets.

Sponsors gain access to filmmakers, artists, technologists, and the finished artifacts that prove what their products can enable.
